THE ALEUTAS: They are perhaps one of the best-known ethnic minorities and one of the ones that have contributed the most with their culture to the current customs of Alaska, since it even owes their name.

Food in Alaska is characterized by being based on fish, the most famous being salmon; their typical meals may contain various shellfish. Other species that are gaining value among Alaskan meals are trout, herring, and crabs.
